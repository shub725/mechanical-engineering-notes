## 📐 Engineering Drawing

### Types of Lines
- Object Line – Thick continuous line
- Hidden Line – Dashed line
- Center Line – Long dash and short dash
- Dimension Line – Thin continuous line with arrows

### Projections
- Orthographic Projection
- Isometric Projection
- Sectional Views

## 🔥 Basic Mechanical Formulas

### Force
F = m × a

### Work
W = F × d

### Power
P = Work / Time

### Torque
T = Force × Radius

### Pressure
P = Force / Area

---

## 📊 Strength of Materials

### Stress
Stress = Force / Area

### Strain
Strain = Change in Length / Original Length

### Young’s Modulus
E = Stress / Strain
